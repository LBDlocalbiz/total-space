Parking Tips

How to Choose the Right Size

Know Your Vehicle Size

Know your vehicle dimensions — length, width, and height. This ensures you pick a spot that fits with room to maneuver.

Go Bigger If in Doubt

If your truck is close to the max for a 9x25 spot, go with the 12x70. The extra space makes parking and pulling out much easier.
