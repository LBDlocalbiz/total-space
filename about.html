About Cleveland Truck Parking

Providing quality truck parking solutions in Cuyahoga County since our founding.

Our Story

Your Trusted Truck Parking Partner in Cleveland

Cleveland Truck Parking offers secure, affordable parking for semi-trucks, tractor trailers, box trucks, and commercial vehicles. Located at 799 E 73rd St in Cleveland, OH with 24/7 access, round-the-clock surveillance, and flexible month-to-month leases.

At Cleveland Truck Parking, we understand that your truck is your livelihood. That's why we've designed our facility with your peace of mind as our top priority — so you can focus on the road, not worrying about where to park.

Whether you're an owner-operator, fleet manager, or local delivery driver, our team is here to help you find the perfect parking solution for your commercial vehicles.
