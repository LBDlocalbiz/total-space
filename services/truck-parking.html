Commercial Truck Parking in Cleveland

Cleveland Truck Parking is your reliable partner for secure commercial vehicle parking in Greater Cleveland. Located at 799 E 73rd St, our facility offers convenient access to I-90, I-77, and I-480 — putting you minutes from Cleveland's busiest freight corridors, distribution centers, and logistics hubs.

Whether you're an owner-operator looking for a safe overnight spot, a fleet manager needing monthly parking for multiple vehicles, or a local delivery driver who needs a home base, we have flexible parking solutions designed for the demands of commercial trucking.

Our facility features wide lanes designed for easy maneuvering of large vehicles, 24/7 gated access, round-the-clock video surveillance, bright LED lighting, and a paved surface maintained year-round. We offer competitive month-to-month leases with no long-term contracts required.
