Tractor Trailer Parking

Professional 12'x70' parking for tractor trailers, 18-wheelers, and over-the-road vehicles in Cleveland, OH.

Tractor Trailer Parking — Full-Size Spots for the Biggest Rigs

Tractor trailers require serious space, and that's exactly what Cleveland Truck Parking delivers. Our 12'x70' parking spots (840 sq ft) are purpose-built for full-size tractor trailers, 18-wheelers, and over-the-road combination vehicles.

The FMCSA estimates that truck drivers spend an average of 56 minutes per day looking for parking. With a reserved monthly spot at Cleveland Truck Parking, that problem disappears. Your spot is waiting for you — guaranteed — whether you roll in at 2 PM or 2 AM.

Our facility sits at the crossroads of Cleveland's major interstates: I-90 (east-west), I-77 (north-south), and I-480 (outer belt). This makes us an ideal staging point for drivers serving the Greater Cleveland metro area, Akron-Canton corridor, and the Lake Erie port region.

Safety is our top priority. Every inch of our facility is covered by 24/7 video surveillance, and our well-lit, paved grounds are designed to minimize blind spots. Wide lanes allow for safe entry and exit without the tight squeezes you'll find at overcrowded truck stops.

We work with owner-operators, fleet managers, and logistics coordinators to provide flexible parking that fits your operation — not the other way around.
