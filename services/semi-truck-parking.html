Semi-Truck Parking — Built for Full-Size Rigs

Finding safe, reliable parking for your semi-truck shouldn't be a daily struggle. At Cleveland Truck Parking, our 12'x70' parking spots (840 sq ft) are specifically designed for full-size Class 8 semi-trucks, 18-wheelers, and over-the-road tractor trailers.

Located near the intersection of I-90 and I-77, our facility is ideally positioned for long-haul drivers running routes through the Great Lakes region, as well as regional carriers serving Northeast Ohio's distribution network. Park your rig securely while you rest, handle paperwork, or wait for your next dispatch.

We understand the realities of life on the road. That's why we offer 24/7 gated access so you can arrive and depart on your schedule — no waiting for office hours. Our wide, paved lanes are designed for easy entry, exit, and backing maneuvers with a fully loaded trailer.

With HOS (Hours of Service) compliance top of mind, having a guaranteed, secure parking spot eliminates the stress of circling truck stops looking for availability. Reserve your monthly spot and know it's waiting for you every time.
